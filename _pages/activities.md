Lausanne Theory for Applied Micro Reading Group (LTAM)
------------
Together with Geoffrey Hilton, I co-founded the Lausanne Theory for Applied Micro (LTAM) reading group. This reading group is aimed at PhD students interested in research that uses economic theory to address empirical questions.  
The reading group now has a website: [LTAM website](https://sites.google.com/view/lausanne-theory-applied-micro/ltam).
